## 📊 Data Points We Deliver

We provide a complete data schema tailored to your specific business needs. **If it’s on Shopee, we can extract it.**

### 1. Comprehensive Product Data
- **Product Listings:** Full titles, detailed descriptions, and category hierarchies.
- **Media Assets:** High-resolution product images and video URLs.
- **Specifications:** Attributes, brand details, and model numbers.

### 2. Pricing & Market Intelligence
- **Dynamic Pricing:** Real-time price monitoring (including original vs. discounted prices).
- **Voucher & Promotion Tracking:** Extraction of shop vouchers, flash sale data, and bundle deals.
- **Historical Trends:** Track price fluctuations and stock movements over time.

### 3. Shop & Seller Analytics
- **Seller Profiles:** Response rates, ship-out time, shop ratings, and total followers.
- **Inventory Tracking:** Real-time SKU-level stock availability.
- **Competitor Monitoring:** Comprehensive shop-wide product exports.

### 4. Customer Sentiment (Reviews)
- **Complete Review Exports:** Ratings, text feedback, and user-uploaded photos/videos.
- **Sentiment Analysis Ready:** Structured data for analyzing customer satisfaction and trends.
