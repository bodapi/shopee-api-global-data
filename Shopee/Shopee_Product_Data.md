### 📦 Shopee Product Data: Strategic Market Intelligence

In the fast-moving Shopee ecosystem, real-time product data is your most valuable asset. Our API doesn't just "scrape" data—it delivers high-fidelity intelligence to fuel your business growth.

#### 🔍 What’s Included in our Product Data?
* **Core Attributes:** Complete titles, multi-level categories, and SEO-optimized descriptions.
* **Media Assets:** Direct access to high-resolution product images and video URLs.
* **Variant Intelligence:** Granular SKU-level data, including price variations and stock availability.
* **Sales Indicators:** Real-time tracking of historical sold counts and current promotion status.

#### 🚀 Business Use Cases:
* **Market Entry:** Analyze trending products before launching in new regions like Brazil or Taiwan.
* **Dynamic Pricing:** Automate your pricing strategy based on competitor price movements.
* **Catalog Automation:** Sync high-quality product data directly into your ERP or own storefront.
